About Us

    Computers for Africa Solutions is a trusted leader in Information and Communications Technology (ICT) across Zambia and the SADC region. We empower organisations by aligning technology with business goals, delivering innovative, end-to-end solutions that drive efficiency, resilience, and growth.

    Our expertise spans ICT infrastructure design, virtualisation, networking, data storage, disaster recovery, compliance, and outsourcing.
